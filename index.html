Julian's Durag Rainbow!

Julian needs your help! You need to help him complete his collection of colorful durags! The layout is pretty simple. You just need to find durags on google and add them to his list so he knows what to buy next! Once you found your image, copy the image address, and paste it into the image URL field. Once you hit submit, the durag you recommended will be added to his shopping list! You can like each durag to show him what you strongly recommend. Also leave a comment on what you think of the durag you uploaded if you had any additional thoughts!
